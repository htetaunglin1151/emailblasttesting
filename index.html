Spread the Word

  • Share the campaign on your social media before each Monday’s email. Use hashtags like #YourDataYourMoney to help spread awareness and encourage others to join.
  • Tag friends, post stories, and encourage everyone to participate.

Set a Schedule

Email Every Monday in January

  • Starting in January, send one email each Monday for four weeks to ensure a steady flow of emails Big Tech companies can’t ignore.
  • Set a reminder or add it to your calendar so you don’t miss a week!
  • Aim to send your email at 12:00 PM EST each Monday. This coordination creates a powerful surge of messages, increasing visibility and response.
